# Using the Privacy Wallet

The Telos Privacy wallet is available at **[zkwallet.telos.net](https://zkwallet.telos.net)**. It lets you move tokens into a shielded pool, transfer them privately, and withdraw them back to any public wallet.

## Three Operations

| Operation | What it does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Deposit** | Move tokens from your public wallet into the shielded pool |
| **Transfer** | Send tokens privately to another shielded account — sender, recipient, and amount hidden |
| **Withdrawal** | Move tokens from the shielded pool back to any public address |

No TLOS is needed for gas — fees are paid in the token being transferred, deducted automatically by the relayer.

## Creating a Shielded Account

Connect your wallet at [zkwallet.telos.net](https://zkwallet.telos.net) and sign the account creation message. This derives your shielded spending key locally — it never leaves your device.

1. Go to the [zkWallet](https://zkwallet.telos.net)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Sign the account creation message — this derives your shielded private key
4. Your shielded account is ready

## Receiving Funds

To receive a private transfer, share your **zk-address** — not your public 0x address. Generate a fresh zk-address for each sender for maximum privacy.

## Fees

Fees are displayed in the app before you confirm any transaction. All fees are paid in the token being used (WTLOS or USDC.e).

For current fee rates, see [zkwallet.telos.net](https://zkwallet.telos.net).
