# Listing & Selling Digital Assets on AreaX NFT

Once you have a minted Digital Asset or an Digital Asset that you own you can visit the Digital Asset's page to list it on the market. 

## Selling Your Digital Asset

1. Open up your Digital Asset's page
2. Click "Sell".

<img
  src={require('/static/img/areax-listing-1.png').default}
  alt="areaX"
  width="70%"
  height="automatic"
/>

3. Set your price.
4. Choose how long you want to list your Digital Asset for.
5. Click "Sell".

<img
  src={require('/static/img/areax-listing-2.png').default}
  alt="areaX"
  width="70%"
  height="automatic"
/>

6. Initialize your wallet by confirming in your wallet pop up.
7. Approve the Digital Asset by confirming in your wallet.
8. Then press confirm.

<img
  src={require('/static/img/areax-listing-3.png').default}
  alt="areaX"
  width="70%"
  height="automatic"
/>

If you've followed all of these steps then your Digital Asset is now listed for sale!
