# Bridging to Telos EVM

## How To Move Assets With The Telos Bridge, Powered By LayerZero

### Step 1

Head to the [Telos Bridge](https://bridge.telos.net/) and press connect at the top right.

<img
    src={require('/static/img/layerzero/1.png').default}
    alt="LayerZero Bridge"
    width="70%"
    height="automatic"
/>

### Step 2

Select MetaMask from the list of wallets and connect your wallet.

<img
    src={require('/static/img/layerzero/2.png').default}
    alt="LayerZero Bridge"
    width="35%"
    height="automatic"
/>

### Step 3

Select the network you're bridging from and the asset you want to bridge. In this case, we are bridging USDT from Polygon.  Next, be sure to choose Telos EVM as the destination.

<div class="three-pics-display">

<img

    width="automatic"
/>

/>

<img
    src={require('/static/img/layerzero/5.png').default}
    alt="LayerZero Bridge"
    height="100%"
    width="automatic"

</div>

### Step 4

Next, add gas on the destination chain if needed.

:::info
If it is your first time bridging to Telos, be sure to select the 'Gas on destination' option, you will receive .05 tlos for free for gas fees.
:::

<img
    src={require('/static/img/layerzero/6.png').default}
    alt="LayerZero Bridge"
    width="35%"
    height="automatic"
/>

### Step 5

From there, you can bridge your assets! You may be asked to switch networks in MetaMask to match the chain you are bridging from if you have not already switched.

<img
    src={require('/static/img/layerzero/7.png').default}
    alt="LayerZero Bridge"
    width="35%"
    height="automatic"
/>

Congrats, your assets are being bridged and will show up in your Telos EVM wallet soon! 

:::info
Your assets will be transferred to the same EVM address that you bridged from. So if you have multiple accounts on MetaMask, just be aware of which one you want your assets to be on.
:::

## How to bridge your TLOS ERC20/BEP20 tokens to Telos EVM using pNetwork

Follow the step-by-step guide below to bridge from Ethereum or Binance Smart Chain to Telos using [pNetwork](https://dapp.ptokens.io/#/swap)!  
The instructions are specifically for ERC20 TLOS on Ethereum, but the steps for Binance are nearly identical, just choose the BSC options instead of the ETH ones.

### Step 1

Head to the [pTokens DApp](https://dapp.ptokens.io/#/swap) Swap Page and connect your wallet

Choose Ethereum, then connect your wallet with Metamask

<img
    src={require('/static/img/ptokens1.png').default}
    alt="Select Ethereum Network"
    width="35%"
    height="automatic"
/>

### Step 2

In the “From” swap section, select TLOS on ETHEREUM.

<img
    src={require('/static/img/ptokens2.png').default}
    alt="Select TLOS on Ethereum"
    width="35%"
    height="automatic"
/>

### Step 3

In the “To” swap section, select TLOS on NATIVE.

<img
    src={require('/static/img/ptokens3.png').default}
    alt="Select TLOS on NATIVE"
    width="35%"
    height="automatic"
/>

### Step 4 

Enter a Telos EVM destination address and make sure “Recieve on a tEVM (Telos EVM) compatible address” is enabled. Enter the amount you would like to swap and complete the transaction.

:::note
If you want to bridge to Telos Zero insead of Telos EVM, toggle off thr "Receive on a tEVM (Telos EVM) compatible address"
:::
