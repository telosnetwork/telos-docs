# Fund Your Wallet

After youâ€™ve created your wallet and added the Telos EVM, it's time to get your hands on some TLOS. The easiest way to purchase TLOS is by using a Fiat onramp such as Topper or Simplex, these solutions allow you to purchase TLOS directly with your credit card. Another alternative is to purchase TLOS on popular exchanges such as KuCoin and Uphold.

## Centralized Exchanges (CEXs)

**When withdrawing from an exchange, please check the available options, and withdraw accordingly.**

:::info
For $TLOS on Telos Zero, we recommend: [Telos Web Wallet](/users/getting-started/setup-wallet#anchor-a-signer-for-telos-zero) withdrawal  
For $TLOS on Telos EVM, we recommend: [Metamask](/users/getting-started/setup-wallet#metamask-a-wallet-for-telos-evm) withdrawal  
:::

:::caution
ERC20 $TLOS is on Ethereum and must be bridged to Telos to have it on the Telos chain, we recommend: Metamask withdrawal, bridge to Telos EVM via [pNetwork](/users/getting-started/bridging/#how-to-bridge-your-tlos-erc20-tokens-to-telos-evm-using-pnetwork)  
BEP20 $TLOS is on Binance Smart Chain and must be bridged to Telos to have it on the Telos chain, we recommend: Metamask withdrawal, bridge to Telos EVM via [pNetwork](/users/getting-started/bridging/#how-to-bridge-your-tlos-erc20-tokens-to-telos-evm-using-pnetwork)
:::

## **Optional:** Link Your Telos Zero Account To An EVM Address

1. Head to [Telos Wallet](https://wallet.telos.net/)
2. Click "Connect Wallet", and sign with Anchor.
3. Click "EVM >>>".
4. Click "Generate Linked EVM Address", and sign with Anchor.

<img
    src={require('/static/img/evm-zero-link.png').default}
    alt="evm wallet setup"
    width="70%"
    height="automatic"
/>

:::info
Now you have a linked EVM address. You can use the Telos Web Wallet to send and receive Telos on the tEVM to that linked address or a Metamask address of your choice!
:::
